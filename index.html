About

Who we are.

DataPrimeTech is a boutique software studio focused on building custom web applications for individuals, small teams, and growing businesses.

We don't sell templates, adapters, or off-the-shelf tools. Every project starts from a blank canvas and is designed specifically around how you and your team actually work — your terminology, your workflow, your edge cases.

We care about craft. That means clean architecture, real security, thoughtful UX, and software that's maintainable long after launch. No technical debt handed to you on day one.

Our products are used daily by real people solving real problems. That's the bar we hold every build to.
